Calibrate Motor

Use the buttons and step selection below to control the motor:

  • Press the + button to move the motor clockwise.
  • Press the − button to move the motor counterclockwise.
  • The Steps dropdown selects how many steps the motor will move with each button press.

Calibration Steps:

  1. Use the + and − buttons to move the motor to the open position.
  2. Click Set Open.
  3. Use the + and − buttons to move the motor to the closed position.
  4. Once the motor is at the closed position, click Set Closed.
Important Notice:

Steps must be adjusted when approaching the limits of open or closed positions to avoid damaging the physical hardware.
